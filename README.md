## Business Insights
The category breakdown shows that some product categories contribute significantly more to overall sales than others.

High-peforming categories represent areas where the compnay has strong customer demand and should continue investing resources.

Decision-makers can use this information to improve inventory planning, allocate marketing budgets, and focus sales strategies on products that generate the most revenue.

One key insight from this dashboard is identifying which product categories drive the most sales. Understanding these difference allows businesses to prioritize high-performing products and make better decisions regarding inventory and marketing strategies.
